Cross country train trip

During winter 2022 I took an Amtrak train trip from the east coast to the west coast, and then back. On the map, blue highlight means that segment is done by train; green highlight means it's done by bus (do not take greyhound). To the best of my memory, the itinerary looks something like this:

  1. Swarthmore Amtrak Station
  2. Philadelphia 30th Street Station
  3. Washington DC Union Station
  4. Chicago Union Station
  5. Denvor Union Station
  6. Salt Lake Central Station
  7. Reno Amtrak Station
  8. Sacramento Valley Station
  9. San Fransisco Salesforce Transit Center
  10. Stockton San Joaquin Street Station
  11. Bakersfield Amtrak Station
  12. Los Angeles Union Station
  13. Flagstaff Amtrak Station
  14. Kansas City Union Station
  15. Chicago >> DC >> Philly >> Swarthmore
