Cross country train trip

During winter 2022-23 I took an Amtrak train trip from the east coast to the west coast, and then back. On the map, blue highlight means that segment is done by train; green highlight means it's done by bus (do not take greyhound). To the best of my memory, the itinerary looks something like this:

  1. Swarthmore Amtrak Station (Dec 17)
  2. Philadelphia 30th Street Station (Dec 17)
  3. Washington DC Union Station (Dec 17)
  4. Chicago Union Station (Dec 18)
  5. Denvor Union Station (Dec 19)
  6. Salt Lake Central Station (Dec 25)
  7. Reno Amtrak Station (Dec 26)
  8. Sacramento Valley Station (Dec 26)
  9. San Fransisco Salesforce Transit Center (Dec 26)
  10. Stockton San Joaquin Street Station (Dec 30)
  11. Bakersfield Amtrak Station (Dec 30)
  12. Los Angeles Union Station (Dec 30)
  13. Flagstaff Amtrak Station (Dec 31)
  14. Kansas City Union Station (Jan 6)
  15. Chicago >> DC >> Philly >> Swarthmore
